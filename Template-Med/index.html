ABOUT US MedClinic centers on diagnosing and treating patients of all ages, while emphasizing preventative medicine and the overall health and wellness of its patients. The clinic features state-of-the-art equipment and trained staff that will optimize the care of each patient. We understand that there are many factors that can affect health, including exercise, diet, and heredity. We are committed to providing the highest quality patient care. Our dedication to excellence, compassion, and innovation is rooted in our devotion to the art and science of healing, which supports every aspect of our mission.
OUR MISSION We aim to provide compassionate, comprehensive, community health care that exceeds our customer's expectations and needs.
OUR VISION The vision of MedClinic is to be the medical services provider that people can trust and rely on regardless of their budget.
MEDCLINIC IS YOUR #1 MEDICAL SERVICES PROVIDER At our clinic, you can get a full range of health care services for you and your family.
OUR SERVICES Our patients have access to a wide range of services right here on-site, as well as off-site through partnerships the Clinic has established. Besides offering great health care, our staff also provides high-quality diagnostics using the cutting-edge medical equipment. Feel free to learn more about our services below.
GENERAL SURGERY Our general surgeons focus on a variety of problems that can be treated with surgery.
OPHTHALMOLOGY PROCEDURES MedClinic’s ophthalmologists provide high-quality eye health procedures to our patients.
OUTPATIENT SERVICES Our outpatient services cover the needs of patients who require follow-up care & check-ups.
RADIOLOGY AND MAMMOGRAMS We provide a wide variety of radiology services including X-ray and mammography.
RESPIRATORY THERAPY Our respiratory therapy services help patients breathe easy whether they're babies or adults.
LABORATORY SERVICES We take great pride in providing the highest quality of laboratory services to our patients.
